30 march. 2022

Ivan Marchuk is a unique Ukrainian artist

Ivan Stepanovich Marchuk is a modern Ukrainian painter, the author of a new style and a unique painting technique in art, called “plentanism” by critics. His paintings seem to be woven from wonderful threads with extraordinary jewelry skill...Ivan Stepanovich Marchuk is the most titled Ukrainian artist and the only Ukrainian who was included in the British rating “One Hundred Geniuses of Modernity”, which was formed by The Daily Telegraph newspaper, and the International Academy of Contemporary Art in Rome accepted Ivan Marchuk into the ranks of the Golden Guild and elected honorary member of the scientific council of the academy (the "Golden Guild" includes 51 artists from around the world). The subjects of his paintings are diverse - folklore and modern ideas, abstract canvases and traditional landscapes.

22 nov. 2018

Kateryna Bilokur. Loneliness in life and obsession in creativity

This woman must have had the soul of a bee. An ordinary person would not be able to see the world as Kateryna Bilokur saw it. More than one guy looked at the black-browed beauty Kateryna. But the girl dreamed of only one thing - to become an artist! Kateryna Bilokur entered our world imperceptibly, and left it, leaving flowers on canvases. Her works were exhibited at a world exhibition in Paris, and Katria lived all her life in the village of Bohdanivka near Yagotyn. About loneliness in life and obsession in the work of Kateryna Bilokur - in the documentary series "The Game of Fate" from the studio "VIATEL" with host Natalka Sopit.

11 apr. 2021

David Hockney. Most Expensive Contemporary Artist

David Hockney (1937 - ....... ) - Painter, graphic artist, photographer, stage designer, living classic of British art, who spent almost 60 of his eighty years building his happy world with brush and canvas.And although his work included both photo collages and drawings on the iPad, images of swimming pools and couple portraits in the interior brought him worldwide fame.At 83, Hockney is considered the most influential British artist and one of the most expensive contemporary artists.
